Travel & Lodging

Air Travel

There are two airports which will work well for travelling to the wedding: Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) and Long Beach Airport (LGB). Both are roughly equidistant to the ceremony location and lodging, 30-45 minutes. LAX is served more regularly by all major airlines, while LGB is primarily served by JetBlue. LGB is a smaller and more manageable airport, but it is recommended to choose a nonstop flight to avoid getting caught in a winter delay during a layover.
