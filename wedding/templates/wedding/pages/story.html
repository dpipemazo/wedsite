Our Story

Love at First Bite

Thanksgiving, 2014. Dan, having recently moved to San Diego, attended "Friendsgiving" on the Friday after Thanksgiving. This is a pot-luck style yearly event typically arranged by Steven Okai that brings together a large group of (mostly Caltech) friends for the holidays. Jennifer, a.k.a "JFang", was in attendance and was attempting to fix a fairly horrible gravy that another friend had started but long since abandoned. Jennifer, introductions be damned, ran up to Dan and asked "what does this gravy need!?". Dan recommended adding some wine, and Jennifer was quite impressed.

The Set-Up

Jennifer, in an effort to cheer up Steven after a recent break-up, hatched a scheme to get Steven a date for Valentine's day. After purchasing Steven a date at the USCD "date auction" fundraiser (yes, you read that correctly), Jennifer was in a bit of a connundrum. She wanted to make it a group date in order to relax the mood, but she herself then needed a date. Out of Steven's San Diego-based friends, Dan was selected as the "goldilocks" of conversational ability. Dan spent the night telling stories of his and Steven's recent blizzard-filled snowboarding adventures, occasionally also delving into some delightful conversation with Jennifer.

The Parking Lot

In the Valentine's day dinner discussions and thereafter through calls and texts, Dan and Jennifer realized they had significant common interests and backgrounds. One of these common interests is the TV show "Avatar: The Last Airbender". A few weeks after the Valentine's day dinner there was an art exhibition dedicated to the show and its fans at an art gallery in Alhambra. Dan invited Jennifer to attend and she obliged. The gallery opening proved to be a popular event, and Dan and Jennifer got to know each other more while standing in line for four hours in the parking lot. The night ended spectacularly with their first kiss!

Dating, sponsored by #Southwest

The long-distance relationship between Jennifer at UCLA and Dan in San Diego seemed reasonable, the the two agreed to give it a shot. Then Dan promptly screwed it up, quitting his job in SD to move into his friend's kitchen in Cupertino and join an early-stage startup. Jennifer was thankfully unphased by Dan's nonsense, and spent a majority of her time those few months on a mattress in said kitchen while studying for Step 1. Over the next few years, Dan and Jennifer ping-ponged back and forth between LA and SF on weekends. Many a brunch was consumed in the dating process.

... and back to the Parking Lot

After toiling through the holidays prepping his CES 2017 demo, Dan managed to take a few days off with Jennifer in LA before his trip to Las Vegas. Jennifer proposed a day trip to see the new Pixar exhibit at the California Science Center and afterwards see the Pixar art expo at the very same art gallery where they first got to know each other. It was pouring rain when they arrived at the gallery, but Dan had other plans. He led Jennifer deep into the parking lot, to the very spot where they had talked for so many hours. Down on one knee, with Jennifer holding the umbrella, Dan proposed and Jennifer said yes!
