Our Story

Love at First Bite

Thanksgiving, 2014. Dan, having just moved to San Diego a few months prior, attended "tygiving," an annual potluck hosted by Steven Okai that brings together a large group of (mostly Caltech) friends for the holidays. Jennifer was attempting to fix a gravy that another friend had started but long since abandoned. Jennifer, introductions be damned, stopped the first person who passed the kitchen (Dan) and asked, "What does this gravy need?!" Dan came to the rescue, and Jennifer was impressed with the fix.

Valentine's Day

Jennifer, in an effort to help their friend Steven, hatched a scheme to get Steven a date for Valentine's Day. After surprising Steven with a date from UCSD's annual date auction fundraiser, Jennifer organized a group date (with help from her bridesmaid Delara Fadavi) to calm Steven's nerves and relax the mood. Out of Steven's three San Diego-based friends, Dan was the "goldilocks" conversationalist, so he came along as Jennifer's date. Dan spent the night telling stories of his and Steven's recent blizzard-filled snowboarding adventures, occasionally also delving into some delightful conversation with Jennifer.

Parking Lot Date

During the Valentine's Day dinner discussions and thereafter, through countless hours of calls and texts, Dan and Jennifer realized they had a lot in common! One of these common interests is the TV show "Avatar: The Last Airbender." A few weeks after the Valentine's Day dinner, there was an art exhibition dedicated to the show and its fans at an art gallery in Alhambra. Dan invited Jennifer to attend its opening night for their first real date. The gallery opening proved to be a popular event, and Dan and Jennifer got to know each other more while standing in line for four hours in the parking lot. The night ended spectacularly with their first kiss!

Dating via Southwest

The long-distance relationship between Jennifer at UCLA and Dan in San Diego seemed reasonable, so the two agreed to give it a shot. Then, Dan promptly upended their budding relationship by quitting his job in San Diego to move into groomsman Sean Keenan's kitchen in Cupertino and join an early-stage startup. Jennifer was supportive of Dan's career, and spent a majority of her time those first few months on an air mattress in said kitchen while studying for her first board exam. Over the next few years, Dan and Jennifer ping-ponged back and forth between LA and SF on weekends. Many a brunch was consumed in the dating process.

...and back to the Parking Lot

After toiling through the holidays to prepare his CES 2017 demo, Dan managed to take a few days off with Jennifer in LA before heading to the conference. Jennifer planned a Pixar-themed date at the California Science Center's interactive Pixar exhibit, followed by a visit to a Pixar art show at the same art gallery where they first got to know each other. It was pouring rain when they arrived at the gallery, but Dan had other plans. He led Jennifer deep into the parking lot, to the very spot where they had talked for so many hours on their first date. Down on one knee, with Jennifer holding the umbrella, Dan proposed and Jennifer said yes!
